Event & Sponsorships

We believe there is a better way to do just about everything in the event and experience arena.

Quadrant Solutions Ltd provides end to end logistics ensuring your events are meticulously planned and designed to operate at maximum efficiency.

Quadrant Solutions Ltd responsibilities include

Detailed planning of the event

To help you get started with this stage we’ve put together comprehensive checklist also divided into stages to work from.

Strategic planning
  • Concept
  • Aims and objectives
  • Evaluate any learnings from the last event
Venue Research

Venue research, coordinating famils and negotiating exclusive venue agreements for the event, hotels, community hall (CIT) and marae.

Venue setup

Venue meets expectations, greeting attendees, and overseeing all vendor activities to achieve seamless events.

Marketing

Activities Promotion of Te Matatini to prospective national and international clients is essential to partnership success.

  • Cash handling
  • Budget monitoring - including claiming expenses

  • Keeping records
  • Bookings
  • Ticketing
  • Cancellations policy
  • Making payments
  • Reporting
