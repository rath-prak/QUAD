Workplace Training

We deliver courses to suit people who are working and want to upskill.

Maori Management – Level 3, 4, 5

This qualification is designed to increase awareness of Te Ao Māori and raise the level of competence and confidence of those working with Māori.

You will learn about:

Working with Te Reo Māori in the workplace, tikanga and the Treaty of Waitangi, building relationships, history of our Ancestors.

First Line Managers

When you achieve this qualification you will have an understanding of: work communication, leadership, team building, decision making and problem solving.

Liquor Controllers Qualification

This course provides you with the two essential unit standards (4646 & 16705) and the legal qualification required by the District Licensing Committee before you can apply for your Manager’s Certificate.
