Who we are

QSOL is a transitional / change consultancy specifically structured to help companies and organisations to achieve key outcomes which are vital to their business.

Our model has been referred to as ‘embedded outsourcing’ as we become, in effect, part of their team - answering queries on behalf of the organisation, managing the finances, as well as dealing with key stakeholders.

Our clients often describe us as the ‘glue’ that holds their organisation together.
